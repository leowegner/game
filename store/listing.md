## Full description (max 4000 chars)

MEGA THONK is a pixel-art survivors-like for tablets. One thumb on the joystick, one on... nothing, because your weapons fire on their own. Your job is to stay alive.

Every run is 15 minutes. Enemies pour in from every side. Pick up XP gems, level up, choose an upgrade. Stack the right upgrades and you're a one-person artillery line. Stack the wrong ones and you're goblin food.

FEATURES
• 50 levels of escalating chaos — the goblins get tougher, the bats get faster, the bosses hit harder.
• 6 weapons that auto-attack: melee hammer, orbiting bonk-orbs, homing seeker bolts, chain lightning, ground-shaking shockwaves, and a constant bleed aura.
• 10 passive upgrade trees — damage, speed, regen, armor, multishot, magnetism, and more.
• ULTRA upgrades — when a weapon or passive is fully maxed, you can transform it: Hammer becomes GODHAMMER, lightning becomes THUNDERGOD, the aura becomes INFERNO.
• A 6-tier permanent shop. Beat a run, bank gold, buy upgrades that carry over. Build the run you want.
• On-screen joystick designed for tablets. Landscape only. No ads. No in-app purchases. No data collection. No internet required.

CONTROLS
• Touch joystick on the left to move.
• Pause button on the right.
• Weapons fire automatically.

If you liked Vampire Survivors, Brotato, or any of the other recent survivors-likes, this scratches the same itch in a smaller package — a complete run is one bus ride.
