# Privacy Policy — Mega Thonk

**Last updated:** 2026-04-26

## What we collect

**Nothing.** Mega Thonk does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The app does not send any information to any server.

## What's stored on your device

The game saves your progress (gold, completed runs, purchased shop upgrades) to your device's local storage so it persists between sessions. This data never leaves your device. Uninstalling the app removes it.

## Permissions

The app requests the `INTERNET` permission. This is requested by the underlying WebView component but the game itself does not make any network requests.

## Third-party services

The app does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking SDKs.

## Children

The app does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children.

## Changes

If this policy changes, the new version will be posted at the URL where you found this one. Continued use of the app after a change constitutes acceptance.
